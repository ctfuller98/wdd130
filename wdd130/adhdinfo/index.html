Main Information on ADHD

Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) is a mental disorder that is extremely common in both children and adults in today's world. Symptoms of ADHD include inattention (not being able to keep focus), hyperactivity (excess movement that is not fitting to the setting) and impulsivity (when you do something without thinking). ADHD is considered a debilitating disorder and is known to impact the individual in many aspects of their life including academics, relationships, and daily functioning. ADHD can lead to horrible self-esteem and social function in children when not treated almost immediately. Adults with ADHD may experience horrible self-worth, may not be able to take criticism as well as others, and will most likely criticize themselves a lot more than others as well.
